Piano

It all started during my last couple years of highschool.

May 25, 2018 at 1:31 PM

I took two semesters of piano when I was still in high school, during my junior and senior year. The first course I took was a beginner level course, and the second was geared towards more advanced students. Unfortunately, I didn't take either class all too seriously since it was just a chill elective for me. However, it wasn't until the summer of my senior year that I actually started caring about playing music. I wasn't sure what got me into it, but it just sort of happened.

Yann Tiersen - La Valse D'Amelie (sheetmusic), featured in the 2001 movie Amelie. I learned this song the summer just before my first year of college in 2013.

Yann Tiersen - Comptine d'autre, also featured in Amelie. I think I learned this song that same summer.


Nowdays, I don't play at all given my lack of freetime, but I would certain like to start again.
