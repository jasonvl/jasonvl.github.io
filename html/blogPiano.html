Piano

I took two semesters of piano once upon a time when I was still in high school. The first course was beginner level, and the second was geared towards more advanced students. Unfortunately, I didn't take either class all too seriously since it was just a chill elective for me. It wasn't until the summer of my senior year that I actually started caring about playing music (not sure why but it just happened...guess it must be the feeling of accomplishment after learning a song). I don't play all too often today given my lack of freetime, but when I do, it's always an enjoyable experience. Enjoy!

Yann Tiersen - La Valse D'Amelie (sheetmusic), featured in the 2001 movie Amelie. I learned this song the summer just before my first year of college in 2013.

Yann Tiersen - Comptine d'autre, also featured in Amelie. I think I learned this song that same summer.
